Why this matters

Breed ID, without the guesswork

Misidentifying a breed affects vaccination schedules, breeding programmes and insurance claims. Field workers often rely on memory or a printed chart. GauLens turns that lookup into a photo scan — useful for extension officers, veterinary camps and dairy cooperatives working across mixed herds.
